EXO

EXO is a South Korean-Chinese boy group formed by SM Entertainment in 2011 that is currently based in Seoul. The group debuted in 2012 with twelve members split into two subgroups‚ EXO-K and EXO-M‚ performing music in Korean and Mandarin respectively. They are considered one of the most popular and successful K-pop groups today and have gained great respect from others.

Fun Fact: The name EXO comes from the word Exoplanet and each members has their own unique super power.

Discography

EXO is known as a triple-million seller in terms of albums‚ eps‚ singles‚ and electronic sales. They were extremely successful with their 3 full length albums&subquo; 5 extended plays‚ and 11 singles.

Since their debut, they have sold more than 32.2 million records in South Korea alone. This includes over 6.5 million physical albums and over 18.6 digital singles as a group along with over 550 thousand physical albums and over 6.7 million digital singles as individual members and subgroups.

Fandom

EXO’s official fandom is called EXO-L which has almost 4 million people. It just goes to show how popular and loved this talented group is.

On their offical fanclub site ‚ you can view their latest music videos‚ behind the scenes clips‚ notices‚ messages from the stars themselves‚ and chat forums.

Fun Fact: The name EXO-L is short for EXO-Love. In alphabetical order‚ L is between K and M. EXO-L is all EXO fans who support EXO between EXO-K and EXO-M and also EXO-L means the fanclub slogan “WE ARE ONE”. EXO and Fan are ONE.
